About Us

Wakad Toastmasters Club

A vibrant community of aspiring speakers and leaders in the heart of Wakad, Pune.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to help individuals overcome the fear of public speaking, Wakad Toastmasters has grown into a warm, encouraging community where members sharpen their communication and leadership skills.

Whether you're preparing for a presentation, levelling up your interview skills, or simply want to become a more confident communicator — we're here to help you grow at your own pace.
